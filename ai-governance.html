The Ethical AI Mandate

Governing the algorithms that protect our world. Clean Blue Green Corp operates under a strict "Human-in-the-Loop" protocol to prevent the weaponization of digital intelligence.

Algorithmic Transparency

Every decision made by our signal-detection AI is logged on an immutable ledger, ensuring auditability for federal and AEAH oversight.

Non-Invasive Processing

Our AI analyzes spectral patterns (frequencies), never private communication data. We monitor the *environment*, not the *individual*.

Anti-Bias Verification

Regular stress-testing of our auditing software to ensure zero bias in environmental restoration and certification issuance.

The "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) Protocol

No EDI Tier 10+ certification is issued by an algorithm alone. Our process requires a certified Human Auditor to verify all AI-generated spectral findings before final accreditation.

  • Step 1: AI Spectral Scan (Initial Detection)
  • Step 2: Forensic Validation (Human Expert)
  • Step 3: Final Attestation (Board Certification)
