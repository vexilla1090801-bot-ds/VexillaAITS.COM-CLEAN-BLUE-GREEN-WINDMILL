Laws of Man vs. Laws of Technology

Our digital environment is as vital as our physical one. AEAH.org is dedicated to protecting the population from electronic abuse, technology misuse, and the harassment of the digital age.

Restoring Digital Ethics

Technology has evolved faster than the laws designed to protect us. When hackers gain access to private data, or when algorithms are designed to cause addiction (doom-scrolling), it is a violation of our Human Environment.

We believe technology should be a tool for achievement, not a weapon for harassment. We study both the "Mental Achievements" enabled by tech and the "Mental Issues" caused by its misuse.

The AEAH Mandate:

  • Defense: Advocacy against electronic harassment and signal intrusion.
  • Education: Teaching the reality of how digital signals affect our biology.
  • Reform: Pushing for technology laws to catch up with the rights of man.
  • Balance: Mitigating the environmental impact of massive data farms.

The Risks (The Abuse)

  • Data Hijacking & Privacy Intrusion
  • Signal Harassment & EMF Overload
  • Addictive Algorithmic Engineering
  • Electronic Stalking & Abuse

The Vision (The Achievement)

  • Secure, Private Infrastructure
  • Human-Centric Digital Limits
  • Educational Empowerment
  • Sustainable Technology Integration
