## 🌎 Vision: "The Environment Cares for Us"
Clean Blue Green Corp is an S-Corporation dedicated to restoring the balance between industrial progress, digital expansion, and environmental preservation. We operate at the intersection of **physical sustainability** (Recycling/Energy) and **digital ethics** (Anti-Electronic Abuse).

Our mission is to provide the "Guiding Light" for entities seeking to standardize environmental excellence and human-centric technology safety.

---

## 🏗️ Core Pillars
The `Clean-Blue-Green-Windmill` repository houses the architecture for our multi-tier ecosystem:

### 1. Industrial & Environmental Auditing
A comprehensive suite of auditing tools for industrial cleaner safety, chemical lifecycle management, and waste-stream optimization.
* **Tiered Audit System:** From consultative reviews to ISO-aligned certifications.
* **Standardized Reporting:** Automated generation of environmental impact scores.

### 2. Sustainable Infrastructure
Engineering frameworks for integrating renewable energy with modern data demands.
* **Renewable Farms:** Management interfaces for Solar and Wind grid integration.
* **Strategic Storage:** Sustainable power storage solutions for Blockchain and Data farms.

### 3. The Bio-Digital Alliance (AEAH.org)
Our unique advocacy arm dedicated to the "Digital Environment."
* **Electronic Abuse Mitigation:** Tools and standards for auditing technology against harassment and misuse.
* **Digital Ethics Audits:** Verifying that corporate algorithms and signals (EMF) are safe for the human population.

## 💎 Proprietary Standard: The EDI Verification
The **Eco-Digital Integrity (EDI)** standard is a 20-tier certification system exclusive to Clean Blue Green Corp. This standard verifies a company's commitment to both the planet and the person.

| Tier | Certification | Focus | Market |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Bio-Safe Workspace | Chemical Safety | Industrial |
| **Tier 5** | Signal-Safe Env | Tech-Harassment | Corporate |
| **Tier 20** | Omni-Stewardship | Global ESG Lead | Enterprise |
