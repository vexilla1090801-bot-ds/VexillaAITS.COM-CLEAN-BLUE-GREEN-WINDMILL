Privacy as an Environment

The Digital Bill of Rights

At Clean Blue Green Corp, we view your data as part of your personal environment. It deserves the same protection as the air you breathe and the water you drink.

01. Zero-Harvesting Policy

We do not sell, trade, or "anonymize" your data for third-party profit. Our revenue comes from audits and infrastructure, not from harvesting our users.

02. Anti-Addiction Interface

Our digital platforms are designed for utility and achievement. We intentionally avoid "Infinite Scroll," predatory notifications, and dopamine-loop engineering.

03. Protection from Signal Abuse

In accordance with AEAH.org standards, we utilize shielded server environments to ensure that data transmission does not contribute to local EMF pollution.

04. The Right to Digital Erasure

Any entity or individual has the "Right to be Forgotten" within our systems. One click, total deletion. No hidden shadows.

05. Transparency of Law

We advocate for the "Law of Man." If a government request for data contradicts our ethical environmental standards, we challenge it openly.
